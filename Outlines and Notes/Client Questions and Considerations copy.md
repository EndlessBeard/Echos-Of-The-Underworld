# Echoes from the Underworld
## Client Questions and Considerations

These are the key questions Rachel and Laura should answer to move the website design from planning into execution.

---

## 1. Brand and Voice

1. What is the final brand name we want to use on the website? Is it definitely `Echoes from the Underworld`, or do we want a supporting tagline to clarify the offer?
2. How do we want to describe our voice in one sentence? Example: "A wise, older-sister energy that is honest, compassionate, and a little playful."
3. What are the exact brand values we want to emphasize on the homepage? (For example: Authenticity, Integrity, Intuition, Compassion, Humor, Groundedness.)
4. Do we want the tone to feel more mystical/ceremonial or more modern/conversational on the homepage?
5. Are there any brand references, eras, or imagery we want the website to explicitly echo (e.g. pomegranate/alchemy, Reputation era, underworld descent)?

---

## 2. Hero and Homepage Messaging

1. What is the one-line value statement for the hero section? This should answer: who we are and what people get.
2. What hero media approach feels best for the launch?
   - Static key art/image
   - Slideshow of imagery
   - Short animated loop / subtle motion
3. Which actions should be most prominent in the hero?
   - Listen to the podcast
   - Join the email list
   - Book a service
   - Visit the shop
4. Do we want a small subheadline in the hero that mentions the podcast cadence and format? (e.g. weekly episodes, tarot insights, shadow work stories.)

---

## 3. Content and Offer Structure

1. Which of these sections are launch-ready?
   - Podcast episodes
   - About Rachel and Laura
   - Blog / newsletter
   - Resources
   - Services
   - Shop / merch
2. Which services will be offered at launch, and what is the short description for each?
   - Speaking engagements
   - Readings
   - Coaching
3. Do we want the website to include a blog archive or just a single featured post at launch?
4. What resource types are most useful to include initially?
   - App/tool recommendations
   - Tarot/astrology quick guides
   - Spiritual and trauma-informed references
5. Do we want a full shop at launch, or a small featured preview with a later expansion?

---

## 4. User Experience and Navigation

1. Is the homepage structure acceptable as a single-page experience with modals for deeper content?
2. Which navigation labels feel best?
   - Podcast, About, Resources, Services, Shop, Contact
   - Or something more branded, like Echoes, Episodes, Guides, Work, Shop
3. Which primary CTAs should appear in the top navigation?
   - Listen Now
   - Book a Session
   - Join Email
4. Do we want a mobile menu drawer or a compact sticky header with a menu button?

---

## 5. Visual and Design Decisions

1. Which color palette direction should we use?
   - Dark, textured underworld tones with red accents
   - Greyscale with pomegranate and green highlights
   - Minimal modern black/white with subtle color cues
2. Are there any visual motifs we want to prioritize?
   - Vines / organic borders
   - Soft torn paper / underworld descent edges
   - Minimal botanical or astrological iconography
3. How much animation should we include?
   - None / static
   - Minimal reveal and hover effects
   - Subtle parallax or motion for the hero and section edges
4. Do we want to use headshots on the About section or more symbolic imagery for privacy and tone?

---

## 6. Legal and Positioning Considerations

1. What exact disclaimer language do we want on the website?
   - Example: "This content is for spiritual and emotional guidance only and is not a substitute for therapy, psychiatric care, or emergency mental health services."
2. How do we want to describe the boundary between our work and therapy/coaching?
3. Should we include a brief statement about referrals to licensed mental health professionals?
4. Do we need a separate page or modal for terms/privacy at launch, or is a footer link enough?

---

## 7. Launch Scope Decisions

1. What is our minimum viable homepage scope?
   - Core podcast + about + contact only
   - Podcast + services + about + resources
   - Full homepage with shop preview and blog teaser
2. What elements can wait until Phase 2?
   - Full blog archive
   - Membership / Patreon page
   - Interactive tarot/astrology tools
   - Full online store
3. What metrics matter most for launch success?
   - Podcast listens / platform links clicked
   - Email signups
   - Service inquiries
   - Contact form submissions

---

## 8. Feedback and Revision Process

1. Who will review the first homepage design draft?
2. How many rounds of content review do we want before launch?
3. Will Rachel and Laura each provide separate feedback, or should feedback be consolidated through one point person?

---

## 9. Optional Creative Questions

1. Do we want an "era" or seasonal theme for the website launch, such as a Reputation-inspired energy or Persephone/underworld season?
2. Would we like a short homepage section to introduce "Hermes" or the guiding spirit energy behind the brand?
3. Do we want to share a gentle statement about the kinds of stories we tell (love, trauma, healing, integration) while keeping personal details safe?
